Policy Detail

This Section Outlines key coverage terms, Exclusions, & indicative pricing to support clear & informed insurance decisions.

What is covered

  • Third-party injury and property damage.
  • Own damage caused by collision or accident.
  • Optional flood and natural peril add-ons.
  • Basic roadside support guidance.

What is not covered

  • Driving without a valid license.
  • Damage from illegal use or racing.
  • Normal wear and tear.
  • Undeclared modifications.
