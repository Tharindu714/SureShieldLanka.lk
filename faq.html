Frequently Asked Questions

Short answers for a non-technical audience.

How do I get a quote?

Use the contact page and send your name, phone number, and vehicle type. A team member can reply with a sample quote.

Can I renew my existing insurance?

Yes. Renewal customers can use the same contact form and mention their current policy expiry date.

What does third-party insurance mean?

It helps cover damage or injury you cause to other people or their property, subject to policy conditions.

Is natural flood cover included?

It can be included as an add-on. This is useful for areas affected by heavy rain or local flooding.

Do you collect sensitive personal data?

No. This demo site only asks for basic contact details needed to respond to a quotation request.

How long does a claim take?

Time depends on the incident and documents submitted, but the claim steps are kept simple and easy to follow.

Is the site usable on mobile phones?

Yes. The layout is designed mobile-first so cards, text, and buttons work well on smaller screens.

Are the prices final market prices?

No. The values shown are sample assignment figures and should be updated with real research if needed.
